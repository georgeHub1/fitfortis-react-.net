The video below provides a fascinating walk through the methodology for dissecting human brains, from someone who has examined more than 1000 brains.

Steve Gentleman -- a professor of neuropathology at the Imperial College in London -- talks about dissecting the human brain. From inspection for infections, tissue softness, atrophy, changes of color, to the reference cuts, to the microscopic analysis.
