Many insitutions and companies around the world are tracking the COVID-19 outbreak. Here are some of the best available online maps of the disease.

Johns Hopkins University    coronavirus.jhu.edu

The COVID-19 map of Johns Hopkins University is probably the most comprehensive map as of this publication. It provides trends for all world countries, detailed per-county trends and infographs for the US, and a number of comprehensive analyses of the critical observed trends.

Bing    bing.com/covid

Microsoft's search engine Bing has rolled out a great COVID-19 tracking tool, with maps and trends for all world countries, as well as pointers to local news articles and videos.

Google    google.com/covid19-map

Similarly to Bing, Google provides a comprehensive COVID-19 portal, with information about the disease, maps, trends, and news.
