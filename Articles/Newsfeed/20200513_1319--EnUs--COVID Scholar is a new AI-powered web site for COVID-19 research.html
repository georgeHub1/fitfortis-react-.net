A team of scientists at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory have created an AI-powered tool for mining of scientific publications related to COVID-19.

The tool, available at covidscholar.org, allows anyone interested in COVID-19 research to search tens of thousands of research papers, using natural language search queries.

The use of AI (artificial intelligence) enables not only powerful search, but also discovery of new scientific knowledge. The latter was demonstrated in a study where an AI-based algorithm was able to predict new discoveries from "latent knowledge" present in scientific publications.
